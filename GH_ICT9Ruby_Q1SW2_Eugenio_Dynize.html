Characters in Noli Me Tangere

Reference for all: Rizal J. (1887) Book: Noli Me Tangere
Don Rafael Ibarra
He was seen as noble and kind man who died in prison. Father of Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin, the main character of the book.
Padre Damaso
A greedy, selfish, boastful and Spanish Friar, Padre Damaso is the main antagolist being Crisostomo's rival.
Don Santiago De los Santos/Kapitan Tiago
He was the first character introduced in the book. His house is known to be open to all, the rich, poor, the Filipinos, Spanish and etc. Malolos is where his house is and he is the child of a rich man.
Tiya Isabel
She is Kapitan Tiago's cousin and she was one of the hosts of their party.
Padre Sibyla
He is currently a Dominican Friar but he was a professor of San Juan de Letran. He is timid and wears glasses.
